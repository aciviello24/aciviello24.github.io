Find our Bitmojis

For the final project, my partner and I decided to make a Find our Bitmoji game modeled after Where's Waldo. On the splash screen you can see each of our Bitmois. Once you click play, the first game screen will appear and you will need to find the Bitmoji to move onto the next level. There is a 10 second timer on each level and if the player finds the Bitmoji before 10 seconds they move on. If the player takes longer than 10 seconds to find the Bitmoji, they have lost the game and an end screen comes up with their final score and a play again button.

To make the game more competitive, we also included a high score feature to keep track of how well the player does each turn.
